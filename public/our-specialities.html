Our Specialities

Health care for your child all under one roof

We are dedicated to providing the highest quality of healthcare for your child and it includes, routine well childcare right from birth to adolescence, treatment for illness and injury and state of the art 24*7 Paediatric Emergency Services. We offer a broad spectrum of inpatient and outpatients services especially tailored to the unique needs of children right from their birth.
